About Me

CandyCrush is my guilty pleasure. I know that statement probably disqualifies me from most development jobs, but the app has taught me how design is essential to the user experience. I want to build real-world applications that are visually appealing and functional. Users should have an enjoyable time with my applications. I want to design, maintain and improve web applications. The applications need to be responsive. I am comfortable with Git/GitHub. I am detail oriented and work well in a team setting. I perform well under pressure and in a fast-paced environment. My projects continue to grow in size and complexity. I am hosting my own cloud-based web-site with AWS. I have written front-end and full-stack apps as well as an API for the oyster lovers of the low-country. My tech stack is javascript (es6), React and node.js and I am proficient in HTML5 and CSS3.

I am available for hire. I also encourage you to reach out with comments about my site. Keep in mind this is a work in progress and I continually try to make it better as a reflection of my coding ability.
